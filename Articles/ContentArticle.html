WHY CATS AND DOGS
ARE DIFFERENT

While cat and dog are the most common pets in the world, there are a lot of common things that make cat and dog so different.
Here is the list of some differences between cat and dog.

  1. Dog is more suitable to become a workout buddy than cat.

  2. While Dog enjoys a game of fetch, cat enjoys pouncing on your feet when you are lying under the cover and sleeping.

  3. Cat are more playful in the evening when dog is active and playful during the day.

  4. Cats are solitary hunters, while dogs are pack members.

  5. Your dog will bark, howl, or growl, whereas a cat meows or snarls.

  6. Cat needs to ingest smaller and more frequent meals than dog.

  7. Cat is quicker to learn using a litter box than dog.

  8. Dog can endure longer training sessions than cat.
